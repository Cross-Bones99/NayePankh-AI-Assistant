# Women Welfare and Empowerment Schemes

## Introduction

Women welfare initiatives aim to improve health, education, safety, financial independence, and social inclusion.

## Key Objectives

* Promote gender equality.
* Improve access to healthcare.
* Encourage education.
* Support entrepreneurship.
* Strengthen financial independence.

## Areas of Support

### Education

Programs supporting girls' education and skill development.

### Healthcare

Initiatives promoting maternal health, menstrual hygiene, and preventive care.

### Financial Inclusion

Programs encouraging banking access, savings, and entrepreneurship.

### Skill Development

Training programs focused on employability and self-employment.

## Menstrual Hygiene Support

Many programs promote:

* Awareness campaigns
* Sanitary pad accessibility
* School-based education initiatives

## Women Entrepreneurship

Support may include:

* Business training
* Financial assistance
* Market access opportunities

## Community Awareness

Awareness campaigns often focus on:

* Health education
* Rights awareness
* Leadership development
* Digital literacy

## Role of NGOs

NGOs can:

* Conduct outreach programs.
* Educate communities.
* Facilitate access to welfare services.
* Connect beneficiaries with government support.

## Volunteer Responsibilities

* Raise awareness.
* Help with documentation.
* Conduct workshops.
* Guide beneficiaries through application processes.

## Success Indicators

* Increased participation
* Improved awareness
* Better health outcomes
* Greater educational attainment
