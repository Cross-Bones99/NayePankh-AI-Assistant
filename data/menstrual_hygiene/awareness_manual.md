# Menstrual Hygiene Awareness Manual

## Introduction

Menstrual hygiene is an essential aspect of women's health and well-being. Unfortunately, many girls and women continue to face social stigma, misinformation, and limited access to sanitary products. Awareness programs help create informed communities and encourage healthy practices.

## Objectives of Menstrual Hygiene Campaigns

* Increase awareness about menstruation.
* Eliminate myths and misconceptions.
* Promote safe hygiene practices.
* Encourage open discussions.
* Improve access to sanitary products.
* Reduce school absenteeism among girls.

## Understanding Menstruation

Menstruation is a natural biological process experienced by girls and women. It is a sign of a healthy reproductive system and should not be considered a disease or source of shame.

### Important Facts

* Menstrual cycles vary among individuals.
* Proper hygiene is important for health.
* Menstruation does not limit a person's capabilities.
* Menstrual products should be changed regularly.

## Common Myths and Facts

### Myth

Women should not attend school during periods.

### Fact

Periods do not prevent learning or participation in educational activities.

### Myth

Menstruation is impure.

### Fact

Menstruation is a normal biological process.

### Myth

Girls should avoid sports during menstruation.

### Fact

Physical activity can be beneficial and safe during menstruation.

## Campaign Planning

### Step 1: Community Assessment

Identify:

* Target audience
* Community concerns
* Existing awareness levels

### Step 2: Resource Preparation

Prepare:

* Presentation materials
* Educational posters
* Sanitary pad samples
* Awareness pamphlets

### Step 3: Volunteer Training

Volunteers should understand:

* Menstrual health basics
* Communication techniques
* Cultural sensitivities
* Common misconceptions

## Conducting Awareness Sessions

### Opening Session

Introduce:

* Purpose of the program
* Importance of menstrual health

### Educational Session

Discuss:

* Menstrual cycle basics
* Hygiene practices
* Product options
* Health concerns

### Interactive Discussion

Encourage participants to:

* Ask questions
* Share concerns
* Discuss challenges

## Sanitary Pad Distribution

When distributing sanitary pads:

* Ensure equitable distribution.
* Explain proper usage.
* Provide disposal guidance.
* Encourage continued hygiene practices.
