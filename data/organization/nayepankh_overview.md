# NayePankh Foundation Overview

## Introduction

NayePankh Foundation is a youth-led non-governmental organization committed to improving the lives of underprivileged communities across India. The organization was initiated during the COVID-19 pandemic when volunteers came together to provide emergency support to people affected by lockdowns, unemployment, hunger, and lack of access to essential resources.

Over time, NayePankh expanded its activities and evolved into a broader social impact organization working in the fields of hunger relief, education, menstrual hygiene awareness, clothing distribution, animal welfare, and community development.

## Vision

To create an inclusive society where every individual has access to basic necessities, education, healthcare awareness, and opportunities for growth.

## Mission

The mission of NayePankh Foundation is to uplift underprivileged communities through volunteer-driven social initiatives that address hunger, education, hygiene, and welfare challenges.

## Core Values

### Compassion

Understanding and addressing the needs of vulnerable communities.

### Integrity

Maintaining transparency and accountability in all activities.

### Inclusiveness

Ensuring equal access to support regardless of gender, age, religion, or background.

### Youth Leadership

Encouraging students and young professionals to contribute to social change.

### Sustainability

Creating long-term solutions rather than temporary interventions.

## Key Focus Areas

### Hunger Relief

Food distribution drives for underprivileged families, homeless individuals, and disaster-affected communities.

### Menstrual Hygiene

Awareness campaigns and sanitary pad distribution programs aimed at improving women's health and dignity.

### Education

Supporting students through educational resources, mentoring, awareness programs, and scholarship guidance.

### Clothing Distribution

Providing clothes and essential items to families in need.

### Animal Welfare

Supporting feeding drives and care initiatives for stray animals.

## Achievements

* More than 2 lakh beneficiaries supported.
* Thousands of volunteers engaged across different regions.
* Numerous awareness campaigns conducted.
* 12A and 80G certified organization.
* Student-led leadership model.

## Volunteer Participation

Volunteers play a critical role in organizing events, conducting surveys, distributing resources, raising awareness, and documenting impact.
